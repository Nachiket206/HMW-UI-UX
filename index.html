HMW Questions & Problem Statement

How Might We Questions

Question 01

How might we improve in-store navigation in Reliance Mart and Decathlon so that customers can locate products quickly and move through the store with ease?

Strategic Approach By understanding user movement patterns and navigation pain points, the experience can be improved through clearer wayfinding, intuitive store layouts, and better visibility of product categories.

Question 02

How might we help customers make confident purchase decisions by simplifying product information and comparisons inside the store?

Strategic Approach Providing clear, easy-to-understand product details and value cues can support users in evaluating options efficiently, reducing cognitive load and uncertainty during product selection.

Question 03

How might we minimize waiting time and frustration during checkout while ensuring a smooth and reliable payment experience?

Strategic Approach By addressing queue management and payment flow inefficiencies, the checkout experience can become faster and less stressful, especially during peak shopping hours.

Final Problem Statement

"Customers at Reliance Mart and Decathlon face challenges in navigating the store, understanding product information, and completing checkout efficiently, leading to confusion and frustration during the shopping journey."
